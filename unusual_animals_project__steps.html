Unusual Animals Project - Steps

In previous sections, we worked with animals’ names and adjectives both comparative and superlative. This is an amazing topic because everyone likes animals and learning about them is very curious. In today's lesson, we're exploring the animal world and find information about peculiar animals. Let’s explore!

1. Real or Fantastic?

There are magical creatures in literature and cinema that can be inspiring, but in real life there are creatures that are just as amazing. In the project, you will find amazing animals, describe characteristics of one of these animals and talk about them in your class.

2. Identify what I have to do

You already know what your challenge is and the activity that will bring you closer to the goal. But to succeed in your journey, you will need some strategies that will help you in this and other similar tasks.

STEP 1. Find info about one usual animal and compile interesting information.

STEP 2. Present the information in the aplication Canva.

3. Simply the best

Each animal is special in a different way. What makes these animals special?  

Think of your favourite animal, what makes it better than the other animals? 

And what about the top ten list of animals you made? Why are they so special?

4. Animal facts

Now, choose one animal you find interesting and write down the information you have.

This is the worksheet where you can write the information about your animal. You can complete this worksheet online, store and download it on your computer. Don’t forget to save the file. If you prefer it, you can also download the worksheet and complete it by hand.
